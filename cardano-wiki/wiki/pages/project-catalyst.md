# Project Catalyst — Innovation Funding

Catalyst is Cardano's community-driven grant program funded from the on-chain treasury. Since 2020 it has distributed **>$150M ADA across ~2,200 projects**.

## Mechanics

1. **Challenges** — broad funding categories proposed by community
2. **Proposals** — anyone can submit; collaboration on Ideascale → on-chain proposal
3. **Voting** — ADA holders vote weighted by stake, via Catalyst Voting App
4. **Funding** — winning proposals draw down ADA in milestones via Lido Nation / Catalyst Milestones

## 2025 → 2026 transition

Stewardship is moving **from IOG → Cardano Foundation**. Funds 15 and 16 paused mid-handover, leaving builders facing a brutal funding gap in early 2026.

The new model proposed for 2026 looks structurally different:
- Targeted tracks instead of broad challenges
- Tighter milestone gating
- Fewer, larger proposals
- More rigorous administrative controls
- Less "lottery", more "structured treasury program"

The trade-off: **less democratic accessibility, more accountability**.

## Tension points

- **Voter apathy & sybil concerns** — voting turnout has been low relative to circulating ADA
- **Delivery rate** — many funded proposals never produce shippable deliverables
- **Value capture** — Cardano funds external projects but rarely retains equity / IP
- **Quadratic vs linear voting** — long-running debate
- **Catalyst vs Intersect Innovation Budget** — different vehicles, overlapping scope

## Adjacent funding mechanisms

- **Innovation Budget** under Intersect Budget Committee
- **DRep-driven info actions** signaling preferences
- **Direct treasury actions** — large ecosystem proposals (e.g. ₳70M integrations fund) bypass Catalyst entirely
